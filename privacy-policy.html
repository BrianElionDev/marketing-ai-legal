Marketing AI – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 19, 2025

1. Information We Access & Use

We do not collect or store personal data from Facebook users directly. However, through Facebook’s API, we may temporarily access:

  • Ad account data (e.g., ad creatives, performance)
  • Page IDs and ad assets shared with us by clients

This data is accessed based on permissions granted when clients share their ad accounts with us through Meta Business Manager.

2. How We Use the Data

Data is used solely to:

  • Create and manage ad campaigns
  • Analyze ad performance for clients
  • Provide automation features through n8n

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, share, or disclose client or Facebook user data with third parties. Data is accessed only via business account integrations provided by our clients and is processed within our automation workflows.

4. Data Retention

We do not store any Facebook user data. Ad performance data may be cached temporarily in our automation system for performance purposes but is purged automatically.

5. Data Deletion Instructions

Clients may request the removal of their data by emailing us at brianeliondev@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

We will revoke access and purge any temporary automation data within 7 business days.
